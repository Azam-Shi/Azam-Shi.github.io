Research Projects

Selected Projects

🧬
BiMba — Binding Site Prediction with Vision Mamba
A novel deep learning framework leveraging the Vision Mamba (state-space model) architecture to predict protein-protein binding sites. Submitted to RECOMB 2026.
🔬
PUMBA — Protein Interface Evaluation
Evaluating protein binding interfaces using advanced vision and sequence models, enabling accurate scoring of docking poses for structural biology applications.
🤖
PIsToN — Transformer-Based Protein Interface Scoring
Applied Vision Transformer networks to evaluate protein binding interfaces, published in Nature Machine Intelligence (IF: 25.89). Developed the PIsToN model and scoring pipeline for Ebolavirus docking.
⚡
CAPRI Competition — Protein Docking Scoring
Participated as a scorer team member in CAPRI Rounds 56, 57, and 60, including the 6th joint CASP-CAPRI assembly prediction challenge — a prestigious blind prediction competition in structural biology.
🌊
Hydrology Time Series Forecasting
Investigated the effectiveness of large time series foundation models for water level forecasting in the Florida Everglades. Contributed to ReFine — a method for boosting prediction under extreme weather events.
🧪
Protein Docking Survey & Benchmarking
Comprehensive survey and benchmarking of scoring functions for protein-protein docking models, published in BMC Bioinformatics 2025. Evaluated AlphaFold2/3, RFDiffusion, ESMFold, and other state-of-the-art models.
